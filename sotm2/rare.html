Rare Plants Garden

The Rare Species Garden is dedicated to the preservation and display of some of the most endangered and unique plant species in the world. Here, visitors can observe plants that are rarely found in the wild, some of which are on the brink of extinction. This garden highlights the importance of conservation efforts and showcases how botanical gardens play a crucial role in protecting biodiversity.
